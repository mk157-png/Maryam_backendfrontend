FAQ

Back to Home

  • How do I create
    an account?

    Click the sign-up button, fill in your basic information, and follow the on-screen steps to finish creating your account.

  • Can I reset my
    password?

    Yes. Go to the login page, click “Forgot Password,” and follow the instructions to receive a reset link.

  • Where can I view
    my data?

    You can view all your data from your dashboard after logging in. Look for the “My Data” or “Account” section.

  • How do I contact
    support?

    Use the “Contact Support” page or send us a message through the help center. We’ll get back to you as soon as possible.
